About Gadiza Handmade

Gadiza Handmade is a business that focuses on making handmade goods and knick-knacks. We create unique and meaningful products, all of which are made with love and attention to detail.

Gadiza Handmade not only produces quality products, but also contributes greatly to creating jobs for local communities. As our business continues to grow, more job opportunities open up, helping to reduce unemployment and improve the economic well-being of the community.

Gadiza Handmade Contribution

Gadiza Handmade also plays an important role in enriching the local economy. By using local materials and involving craftsmen from various backgrounds, we help drive the wheels of the local economy and increase people's income.

Our efforts also encourage sustainable economic growth by creating environmentally friendly products and supporting fair business practices. Thus, Gadiza Handmade not only creates products, but also provides a broad positive impact on society.

How can we help you?

We hope you have found an answer to your question. If you need any help, please search your query on our Support Center or contact us via email.

To change your billing information, please follow these steps:

  • Go to our website and sign in to your account.
  • Click on your profile picture in the top right corner and select "Account Settings."
  • Under "Billing Information," click on "Edit."
  • Make your changes and click on "Save."

A payment system is a way to transfer money from one person or organization to another. It involves banks, credit card companies, and merchants.

To cancel your account, please follow these steps:

  • Go to our website and sign in to your account.
  • Click on your profile picture in the top right corner and select "Account Settings."
  • Scroll to the bottom and click on "Cancel Account."
  • Enter your password and click on "Cancel Account."
